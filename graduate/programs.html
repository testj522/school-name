The best way to take your career to the next level is by earning an advanced degree.

The School of Graduate Studies offers graduate programs that meet the needs of an ever-changing economy. Whether you choose to move into the teaching field, specialize in nursing, or become an entrepreneur, we have the right program for you. Our university has become a leader in education in metro Atlanta by supplying the student community with an award-winning faculty, a convenient location, and flexible programming.

Meet your programs directors

Master of Archival Studies

Study archival theory and practice with an emphasis on appraisal and description, arrangement and description, reference and access, outreach, preservation, and management of records in all formats.

Learn More about the Master of Archival Studies

Master of Arts in Liberal Studies

This interdisciplinary program encourages innovative thought, promotes connections to others, and cultivates analytical reasoning skills, characteristics that most employers look for from job seekers.

Learn More about the Master of Arts in Liberal Studies

Master of Arts in Teaching

Students will become professional educators who engage in reflective practice and are competent, caring, committed, collaborative, culturally responsive, and prepared to teach diverse learners in an ever-changing society.

Learn More about the Master of Arts in Teaching

Master of Business Administration

Gain a better understanding of the importance of ethical business behavior and corporate social responsibility to lead organizations in a business environment that is global and rapidly changing.

Learn More about the Master of Business Administration

Master of Health Administration

Professionals from both health and non-health related areas will learn the skills necessary for entry and mid-level management and leadership positions in a variety of health service organizations in the changing health service system

Learn More about the Master of Health Administration

Master of Public Administration

Build foundational skills in local government administration, research and applied statistics, human resource management, ethics, and legal issues to play a greater role in crafting and carrying out sensible public policy.

Learn More about the Master of Public Administration

Master of Science in Nursing

Registered nurses can advance their careers to become culturally competent primary providers for adult, pediatric and geriatric patients and their families.

Learn More about the Master of Science in Nursing

Master of Science in Clinical/Counseling Psychology

Develop skills in psychological assessment and in a variety of therapeutic modalities, including individual, family, couples, and group therapy, to become an ethical practitioner of psychological services in the community.

Learn More about the Master of Science in Clinical/Counseling Psychology

Post-Master’s Certificates

Sometimes you need some additional knowledge and skill in a specific area to enhance your career. We offer several certificates in business and health fields for individuals who already possess a bachelor’s degree.

Learn more about the Post-Master's Certificates.

  • Family Nurse Practitioner
  • Human Resource Leadership, International Business, Digital Marketing, and Supply Chain Management
